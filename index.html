Project Introduction

This project mainly identifies the specificity of microplastics in the environment through spectral analysis. And through plasmid construction, microbial culture and purification, protein purification, and multi enzyme chimerism, innovative multi enzyme system modification was carried out, and molecular docking was used to evaluate and predict the feasibility, ultimately upgrading PET to reconstruct protocatechuic acid with rich pharmacological effects.
